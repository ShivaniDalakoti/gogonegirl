Our Story

Meet the Founders

We built Go Gone Girl because the world needed it.

Three friends — Priya, Neha, and Aditi — met in college and bonded over a shared dream: to travel the world on their own terms. After years of solo trips, group adventures, and late-night travel planning, we realized something was missing in the travel industry. Most companies catered to couples or generic group tours. Women travelers deserved better.

Go Gone Girl was born from frustration with overpriced tours, unsafe experiences, and the constant need to compromise on what we really wanted. We wanted curated, women-only experiences that didn't sacrifice on safety, comfort, or adventure. Experiences built by women, for women.

Today, we've sent over 500 women on unforgettable journeys across India and beyond. But we're just getting started. Every trip is a chance to prove that women can travel freely, safely, and authentically — on their own.

Himalayan Escape: Nainital, Kainchi Dham & Corbett

Price: ₹20,000 per person (MAP Plan)

Day 1: Arrival & Ramgarh Slow Living

Pickup from Kathgodam. Scenic drive to Ramgarh. Check-in at Blue Mountain Homestay. Unwind with sunset views and optional bonfire.

Day 2: Nainital & Kainchi Dham Exploration

Explore Naini Lake, Mall Road, local cafés. Visit Kainchi Dham (Neem Karoli Baba Ashram). Return to Ramgarh by evening.

Day 3: Mukteshwar & Bhalugaad Waterfall Trek

Visit Mukteshwar's cliffside viewpoints. Trek down to refreshing Bhalugaad Waterfall.

Day 4: Ramgarh to Jim Corbett

Check out after breakfast. Scenic drive to Corbett. Guided nature walk in the evening.

Day 5: Early Morning Safari & Departure

Rise early for open Jeep Safari inside Corbett National Park. Return for breakfast, check out, and drop back.

Jim Corbett Weekend Wilderness

Price: ₹10,000 per person

Arrive by noon. Hearty lunch followed by guided nature walk along Kosi River. Bonfire in evening.

Day 2: Open Jeep Safari Day

Early morning tiger reserve safari. Relaxed afternoon by pool. Evening games and bonfire.

Day 3: Forest Trails & Departure

Slow morning breakfast. Visit Corbett Museum before heading back to station with unforgettable memories.

Kerala Backwaters & Hills

Price: ₹25,000 per person

Day 1: Cochin to Munnar Arrival

Pick up from Cochin. Visit Cheeyappara waterfalls. Check in at Munnar hotel.

Day 2: Full Day Munnar Sightseeing

Visit Eravikulam National Park, Tea Museum, Mattupetty Dam, Echo Point.

Day 3: Munnar to Thekkady

Drive to Thekkady. Visit Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary and spice plantation.

Day 4: Thekkady to Alleppey Houseboat

Head to Alleppey (Venice of the East). Houseboat stay on backwaters.

Day 5: Alleppey to Cochin Heritage

Cochin heritage walk. Jew Street, Synagogue, Dutch Palace, Chinese Fishing Nets.

Day 6: Departure

Transfer to Cochin Airport or Railway Station for return journey.

Sikkim & Darjeeling Queen of Hills

Price: ₹20,700 per person

Day 1: Arrival Journey to Gangtok

Meet driver at Bagdogra. Scenic transfer to Gangtok hotel for rest.

Day 2: High-Altitude Tsomgo Lake

Drive to 12,400 feet. Visit sacred Tsomgo Lake and New Baba Mandir shrine.

Day 3: Gangtok Sightseeing & Darjeeling Transfer

Tour Flower Show, Dro-dul Chorten, Ropeway. Transfer to Darjeeling.

Day 4: Tiger Hill Sunrise & Local Sights

Dawn Tiger Hill sunrise over Mt. Khangchendzonga. Visit Ghoom Monastery, Batasia Loop.

Day 5: Darjeeling Departure

Enjoy breakfast over tea valleys. Return drop to Bagdogra Airport.

Meghalaya & Assam: Clouds & Canyons

Price: ₹18,800 per person

Day 1: Guwahati to Shillong

Pick up and drive to Shillong, stopping at Umiam Lake. Explore Police Bazaar street market.

Day 2: Cherrapunjee Waterfalls

Visit wettest place on earth. Nohkalikai Falls, Mawsmai Cave, Eco Park, Seven Sisters viewpoints.

Day 3: Cleanest Village & Dawki River

Visit Mawlynnong. Cross Living Root Bridge. Transparent boating on crystal-clear Umngnot River.

Day 4: Shillong to Guwahati

Drive down to Guwahati. Visit historic Kamakhya Temple. Sunset walk.

Day 5: Departure Day

After breakfast, transfer to Guwahati Airport or Railway station for journey home.
