## 🎯 WHAT IS GONE GIRL?

Gone Girl is a **women-first travel community platform**, not a booking site.

### What you can do:
- **Browse curated trips** for women travelers (mountains, beaches, wildlife)
- **See the community** — Reviews and photos from women who went before
- **Book with confidence** — You're not paying for logistics; you're joining a group of vetted women
- **Connect before you go** — Chat, share photos, build relationships with your travel group
- **Manage your trips** — Dashboard shows all your bookings and community connections

### Why it's different:
- **Trust-first design** — Ratings, reviews, and verified women travelers are front-and-center
- **Community embedded** — Not bolted-on social features; it's the core product
- **Women-centric** — Designed by women, for women, with safety and belonging as the default

## 💰 THE WEDGE

We don't compete on price. We compete on **trust**.

- Competitors: Airbnb, Booking.com, Intrepid, G Adventures (generic platforms)
- Our advantage: A community of verified women where safety and belonging are the default

**Defensibility:**
1. **Community lock-in** — Once you've traveled with women via Gone Girl, you come back
2. **Network effects** — More women = more trips = more trust = more women
3. **Brand** — "Gone Girl" means something to women travelers. It's a movement, not a feature.
