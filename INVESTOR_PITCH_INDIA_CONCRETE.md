## PART 3: PLATFORM REVENUE

### How Gone Girl Makes Money

**Revenue Model: 18% Platform Commission**

Gone Girl doesn't charge customers ₹3,500. We charge the hotel/service providers.

| Layer | Amount | Who Pays |
|-------|--------|----------|
| **Raw trip cost** | ₹32,000/person | Customer pays |
| **Platform commission (18%)** | ₹5,760/person | Hotel/services pay us |
| **Group total revenue** | ₹86,400 | From 15-person group |

**Why 18%?**
- Industry standard for OTA platforms (MakeMyTrip: 20%, Booking.com: 15-20%)
- Covers operations: customer support, payment fees, platform infrastructure
- Sustainable for profitability by Year 2
