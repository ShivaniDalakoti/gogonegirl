**What it costs:**
- Flight: ₹4,000
- Hotel: ₹20,000
- Meals: ₹3,000
- Activities: ₹4,000
- Misc: ₹1,000
- **Total: ₹32,000/person**

**What we charge:**
- Markup: ₹3,500 (11% for curation + community)

**What we make:**
- Group of 15: ₹5,32,500 gross

**Margin: 86% per trip** ✓ (Realistic)
