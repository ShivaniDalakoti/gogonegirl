# Gone Girl - 2 Minute Founder Pitch Script

## 🎬 COMPLETE SCRIPT WITH TIMING

## [0:00-0:12] HOOK (On Camera - You Speaking)

*Look directly at camera. Confident, clear, conversational.*

**"Hi, I'm Shivani. Women travelers spend over $100 billion a year, but every platform treats travel like a commodity transaction—anonymous, transactional, lonely.**

**We built Gone Girl to change that."**

---

## [0:12-0:25] THE PROBLEM (Voiceover + Home Page)

*Screen shows Gone Girl home page: hero section + testimonials playing*

**"The problem isn't that women don't want to travel. It's that they want to travel with other women they trust. They want community. They want safety. They want sisterhood.**

**But Airbnb, Booking.com, every platform treats trips like a logistics problem, not a social one."**

---

## [0:25-0:40] THE SOLUTION - HERO FLOW (Screen Demo)

*Click "Explore Trips" button → Trips page loads*

**"We built Gone Girl differently. It's not a booking site with community bolted on. It's a community platform built on top of trips."**

*Filter shows 4 trips: Himalayan Escape, Kerala Backwaters, Jim Corbett Wilderness, Sikkim & Darjeeling*

**"Browse trips by type—mountains, beaches, wildlife. Each trip isn't just an itinerary. It's a group of vetted women you'll travel with."**

---

## [0:40-0:50] TRIP DETAILS + COMMUNITY (Click One Trip)

*Click on "Himalayan Escape" → Trip Details page loads*

**"Click into a trip and you see the full picture: itinerary, pricing, but also—the community. You see who you're traveling with. You see photos from past trips. You read reviews from real travelers."**

*Show the community gallery and testimonial videos*

**"This is the part other platforms hide. We put it front and center."**

---

## [0:50-1:05] BOOKING FLOW (Click "Book Now")

*Navigate to Booking page → Show multi-step booking*

**"The booking is simple. But it's not anonymous. You're not booking a hotel room. You're joining a group. You're paying to be part of a community of women explorers."**

*Show booking form with trip details and pricing*

---

## [1:05-1:20] USER DASHBOARD (Login → Dashboard)

*Show login, then navigate to Dashboard*

**"Once you book, you have a dashboard. You see all your trips, your community, your fellow travelers. You can chat before you leave, share photos, build relationships."**

*Show dashboard with bookings and community features*

---

## [1:20-1:35] THE WEDGE - WHY THIS WORKS (Back to You, On Camera)

*Cut back to you on camera*

**"Here's why this works: trust is the highest-margin feature in travel. Women don't want the cheapest flight to Bali. They want to go with people they trust, in an environment designed for safety and sisterhood.**

**Every competitor optimizes for price. We optimize for belonging."**

---

## [1:35-1:50] TRACTION + FOUNDER STORY (Home Page - About Us Section)

*Show About Us section with founder photos*

**"We're Shivani and Aanshi—two founders who've built this product and tested it with real travelers. We have testimonials, real bookings, and a waiting list of women ready to go.**

**We're not pitching an idea. We're pitching proof that works."**

---

## [1:50-2:00] VISION + CTA (Final Camera Shot)

*Back to you, camera, confident close*

**"Travel is one of the few spaces where women outnumber men as consumers but have zero platforms built for them. We're building the social network for women travel.**

**In five years, when a woman thinks about booking a trip with her community, she thinks Gone Girl."**

*Smile, pause, let it land*

**"Let's go."**

## 🎯 KEY POINTS TO NAIL

1. **Open with conviction** — You see something others don't. Say it clearly.
2. **Show, don't tell** — Let the product do the talking during the demo.
3. **Community is the wedge** — Not features, not price. Why women *trust* you.
4. **Proof of execution** — You shipped this. That matters more than slides.
5. **Close with vision** — Leave them imagining the future.

---

## 📝 TONE NOTES

- **Conversational, not rehearsed** — Sound like a founder in a coffee meeting, not a pitch deck presenter
- **Confident but not cocky** — You've built something real; let it speak
- **Specific examples** — Use the trip names, real numbers, real communities
- **Pause for impact** — After key statements, let silence land

---

## 🎥 FILMING CHECKLIST

- [ ] Good lighting (natural or ring light)
- [ ] Clear audio (phone mic is fine, avoid background noise)
- [ ] Eye contact with camera for on-camera segments
- [ ] Steady screen recording (use QuickTime or ScreenFlow on Mac)
- [ ] Demo the app at normal speed (no rushing)
- [ ] Pause after booking shows, let audience see the details
- [ ] Close with confidence

---

## 📹 HOW TO STRUCTURE THE VIDEO

1. **Record on-camera segments separately** (hook at 0:00, wedge at 1:20, vision at 1:50)
2. **Record screen-capture demos** (home page → trips → trip details → booking → dashboard)
3. **Add voiceover during demos** (using Voiceover in QuickTime or separate audio editor)
4. **Assemble in iMovie/CapCut** (intercut on-camera and screen capture)
5. **Add light music** (subtle, royalty-free from YouTube Audio Library)
6. **Export at 1080p** (submit as MP4)

---

## 💡 PRO TIPS

- **Start filming tomorrow** — Don't overthink. The first take is usually best.
- **Smile before you speak** — It comes through in your voice.
- **Practice the hook once** — Then just record. Authenticity > Perfection.
- **Show real data if you have it** — Waitlist signups, emails, beta feedback. Anything real beats polish.
- **Test the app before filming** — Make sure all clicks work, no loading errors.

**Most importantly:** Founders who pitch to Amit show they can *ship*, not just dream. You've shipped this. Let that be your superpower.
