# Gone Girl Pitch - Quick Reference (Print This)

## 📝 THREE SCRIPTS TO MEMORIZE

### HOOK (12 sec) - On Camera
> "Women travelers spend over $100 billion a year, but every platform treats travel like a commodity transaction—anonymous, transactional, lonely. We built Gone Girl to change that."

### WEDGE (15 sec) - On Camera
> "Trust is the highest-margin feature in travel. Women don't want the cheapest flight. They want to go with people they trust. Every competitor optimizes for price. We optimize for belonging."

### VISION (10 sec) - On Camera
> "Travel is one of the few spaces where women outnumber men as consumers but have zero platforms built for them. We're building the social network for women travel. In five years, when a woman thinks about booking a trip with her community, she thinks Gone Girl. Let's go."

## 🎬 FILMING CHECKLIST

### BEFORE YOU START
- [ ] Good lighting (window or ring light)
- [ ] Quiet room (no AC, pets, background noise)
- [ ] Phone propped at eye level
- [ ] Backend running: `npm start` (root)
- [ ] Frontend running: `cd app && npm run dev`
- [ ] App loads: http://localhost:5173
- [ ] All 4 trips visible
- [ ] Test: Trip clicks → Details page loads
- [ ] Test: "Book Now" → Booking form shows

### ON-CAMERA (Do 3-5 takes each)
1. **Hook** — Eye contact, confident, pause at end
2. **Wedge** — Slower, more conviction
3. **Vision** — Inspiring, decisive, smile at end

### SCREEN RECORDING
1. Start on home page
2. Scroll to show testimonials & gallery
3. Click "Explore Trips"
4. Wait for load, pause 1 sec
5. Click "Himalayan Escape"
6. Wait for load, scroll to show reviews
7. Click "Book Now"
8. Wait for load, show form, pause 2-3 sec
9. Stop recording

**Total: ~75 seconds**

### VOICEOVER
Read over demo clips. Timing should match video.

### ASSEMBLY (iMovie/CapCut)
1. Drag hook.mp4
