# Gone Girl - Investor Pitch Deck

## Executive Summary

**Gone Girl** is a women-first community travel platform that transforms how women travel by prioritizing trust, safety, and belonging over price and logistics. We're building the social network for women travelers—a defensible market with zero direct competitors.

**The Opportunity:** $100B+ annual spend by women travelers; only 0.1% served by women-centric platforms.

---

## 1. CORE PROBLEM

### The Gap: Women Travel Differently Than Platforms Assume

**Problem Statement:**
Women represent 52%+ of international travelers and $100B+ in annual travel spend, yet travel platforms (Airbnb, Booking.com, Intrepid, G Adventures) treat all travelers identically. They optimize for:
- Price
- Logistics
- Transaction completion

**What women actually want:**
- Trust & safety verification
- Community & belonging (not solo logistics)
- Authentic experiences with like-minded women
- Verified travel companions
- Shared decision-making (not solo bookings)

**Market Validation:**
- 72% of women travelers cite "feeling safe" as #1 travel priority
- 68% would pay premium for women-verified travel communities
- Women solo travelers grew 60% post-COVID (vs. 12% male growth)
- $0 in venture funding → women travel startups (massive gap)

**Why existing solutions fail:**
| Platform | Model | Why It Fails for Women |
|----------|-------|------------------------|
| Airbnb | Peer-to-peer rentals | Anonymous, safety concerns |
| Booking.com | Hotel aggregator | Transactional, no community |
| Intrepid/G Adventures | Generic tour operators | One-size-fits-all groups |
| Bumble BFF | Friend networking | Not travel-focused |
| Female Founders Fund | Accelerator | No travel product |

**The wedge:** No platform lets women *see* other women, build trust, and travel together as the core product mechanic.

## 6. BUSINESS MODEL

### Revenue Streams

**Primary: Commission on Bookings**
- Trip price: $2,000-$5,000 per person (typical)
- Take rate: 15-20% (platform commission)
- Example: $3,000 trip × 10 women × 18% = $5,400 per trip
- Frequency: 50+ trips/month by Year 2 = $270K MRR

**Secondary: Premium Features (Optional)**
- Verified badge for women (early adopter)
- Enhanced profile visibility
- Early access to trips
- Premium community tools
- Projected: 10-15% of users × $5-10/month = 2-3% of revenue

**Tertiary: Partnerships & Adjacencies** (Year 2+)
- Women-focused travel insurance
- Travel gear recommendations
- Content partnerships (blogs, podcasts)
- Corporate team-building packages
- Brand partnerships (hotels, airlines seeking women travelers)

### Unit Economics (Projected)

**Per Booking:**
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Trip price | $3,000 |
| Commission (18%) | $540 |
| Platform cost (payments, ops) | ($80) |
| Gross profit per booking | $460 |
| Gross margin | 15.3% |

**Per User (Year 1-2):**
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| CAC (organic + paid mix) | $25-50 |
| LTV (3 trips/year × 2 years) | $1,380 |
| LTV:CAC ratio | 27:1 |
| Payback period | 2 weeks |

**Unit economics are highly favorable.** This is a multiplier business.
